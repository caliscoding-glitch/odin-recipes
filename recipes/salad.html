Halloumi Salad

Salad recipe.

Crisp savoury salad

A delicious home made salad with a pan seared halloumi bites

Ingredients

  • Avocado 1/2
  • Red onion 1/4
  • Tomato 1
  • Black olives 1/2 cup
  • Pumpkin seeds 1/2 cup
  • Spinach leaves
  • Salad leaves, seasonal

Method

  1. Dry pan roast pumpkin seeds
  2. Cut the avocado into meduim sized cubes.
  3. Chop onion into fine dices.
  4. Chop Olives into fine slices
  5. Chop tomotoes into small cubes.
  6. Finely slice spinach, place in bowl
  7. Add seasonal salad leaves into bowl after rinsing in cold water.
  8. Add copped ingredients to salad leaves
  9. Sprinkle pumpkin seeds on top of salad
  10. Serve

Enjoy!
