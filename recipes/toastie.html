Toastie Pie

Pan fried toastie recipe

Crispy cheese toastie

A crispy and melty treat for a cold afternoon, or sneaky treat.

Ingredients

  • Ciabatta rolls cut in half 2
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil pesto
  • butter
  • onion
  • sun blushed tomatoes

Method

  1. Slice ciabatta rolls in half
  2. Butter one side
  3. Slice onion and tomatoes
  4. Grate mozza
  5. Preheat pan
  6. Place ciabatta butter side down in pan
  7. Spread pesto on bread.
  8. Place onion and tomato on top of pesto
  9. Spread pesto on second piece of ciabatta
  10. Place second piece of bread butter side up
  11. Press down until you hear a sizzle.
  12. Leave for two mins
  13. Flip, cook a further 3 mins

    14. Done! Enjoy.
