Delicious Spicy Salsa

A delicious home made salsa with a bit of spice and bite to complement any summer meal.

Ingredients

  • Avocado 1
  • Red onion 1/2
  • Tomato 1
  • Black olives 1/2 cup
  • Jalapenos handful (optional)
  • Lime juice 2Tbspn
  • Salt n Pepper

Method

  1. Mash the Avocado in a bowl with a fork.
  2. Chop onion into fine dices.
  3. Chop Olives and Tomotoes into cubes.
  4. Chop Jalapenos, if using.
  5. Add chopped ingredients to avo.
  6. Stir with a fork-like mashed potatoes.
  7. Add Lime juice.
  8. Add a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste.

Enjoy!
