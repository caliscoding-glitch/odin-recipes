Delicious Spicy Salsa A delicious homed made salsa with a bit of spice and bite to complement any summer meal.

Ingredients

  • Avocado 1
  • Red onion 1/2
  • Tomato 1
  • Black olives 1/2 cup
  • Jalapenos handful (optional)

  • Lime juice 2Tbspn
  • Salt n Pepper
Method 1. Mash the Avocado in a bowl with a fork 2. Chop onion into fine dices 3. Chop Olives and Tomotoes into cubes 4. chop Jalapenos, if using 5. Add chopped ingredients to avo 6. stir with a fork-like mashed potatoes 7. Add Lime juice 8. Add a sprinkle of salt and pepper to taste. Back to Recipes
